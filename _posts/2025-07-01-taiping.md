## Project experience:

At [Taiping Life Insurance](https://www.ctih.cntaiping.com/), I cleaned and aggregated time-stamped claim data to identify workflow patterns. I created hourly distributions of claim completion times and found operational bottlenecks that helped my supervisor better understand workflow inefficiencies. In another project, I discerned abnormal cases and their common characteristics from a massive datasheet with thousands of claim records. After cleaning the data and isolating the cases with abnormally low insurance reimbursement ratios, I discovered many abnormal cases were concentrated in certain hospitals; I also conducted statistical tests to determine whether specific expense types were higher for abnormal cases. From this internship, I became accustomed to handling large, messy datasets and extracting insights that inform decision-making, and I look forward to advancing my statistical and computational techniques further.
